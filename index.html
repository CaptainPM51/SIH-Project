How To Keep Animals Safe from Heatwave?

Keeping animals safe during a heatwave is crucial, as extreme heat can be just as dangerous for them as it is for humans.Remember that different animals have different heat tolerance levels, so it's important to tailor your approach to the specific needs of the animals you are caring for.
